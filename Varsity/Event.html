the AAST Center of Entrepreneurship 15 October 2019 87

About Entrepreneurship Rally.

The Arab Entrepreneurship Rally is a competition powered by the AAST Center of Entrepreneurship in the Arab Academy for Science, Technology, and Maritime Transport for people who want to start their own business. It is held in partnership with UNIDO ITPO, AICEI, Union of Arab Chambers, and Bahrain Chamber of Commerce and Industry. University students from many countries in the Arab region will get the chance to discuss their business plan with many experts in their field of business, and attend different entrepreneurship events.

Over the past 2 years, we have had a total of 5,300 participants in the program, all within a range of 18 to 35 years. We have accepted more than 370 students’ ideas, and included them in more than 90 educational events and trainings.

Competition Stages.

  • Competition Deadline 15/10
  • Technical Judging 01/10 to 19/10.
  • Identification of top teams 20/10
  • Bootcamp (Held in Bahrain) 07/11 to 09/11.
  • Final and VIP Judging 10/11.
  • Awarding Ceremony 12/11.
